Colombia

Colombia, officially the Republic of Colombia, is a sovereign country located in the northwestern part of South America; its capital and largest city is Bogotá. It is bordered to the north by the Atlantic Ocean, to the east by Venezuela and Brazil, to the south by Peru and Ecuador, and to the west by the Pacific Ocean and Panama. Colombia has Pacific coastlines and access to the Atlantic through the Caribbean Sea, where it includes several islands such as the archipelago of San Andrés, Providencia, and Santa Catalina.
